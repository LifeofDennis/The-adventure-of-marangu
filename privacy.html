Privacy Policy for The Adventure of Kilimanjaro

Last updated: October 26, 2025

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website and tour inquiries.

Information we collect

We may collect contact details, trip preferences, booking details, and safety-related information needed to plan treks and tours.

How we use information

We use information to answer inquiries, prepare itineraries, coordinate bookings, support guides and partners, improve the website, and communicate trip updates.

Sharing

Information may be shared with trusted guides, transport providers, accommodation partners, and relevant authorities only when needed to operate your trip.

Security and rights

We use reasonable safeguards and you may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your information where applicable.
