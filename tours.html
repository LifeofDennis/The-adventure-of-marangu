Kilimanjaro climbing routes

Compare the two main climbing options and choose the route that fits your style.

Marangu Route

The classic “Coca-Cola” route with mountain hut accommodation and a direct route profile.

  • 5-6 days
  • Mountain huts
  • Moderate difficulty
  • Starts at Marangu Gate

Best for

Travelers who prefer hut accommodation, classic scenery, and a shorter trek option.

Book Marangu

Machame Route

The scenic “Whiskey” route with camping, varied landscapes, and strong acclimatization opportunities.

  • 6-7 days
  • Tented camps
  • Moderate to challenging
  • Starts at Machame Gate

Best for

Trekkers who want a more scenic route and do not mind camping and steeper sections.

Book Machame
