Marangu Route ("Coca-Cola Route")

Kilimanjaro's most popular hut-to-hut climbing route

Marangu Route Overview

The Marangu Route is one of the most popular routes for climbing Mount Kilimanjaro, the highest peak in Africa. Here are some key details about this route:

Starting Point

Marangu Gate

Ending Point

Marangu Gate

Duration

Typically 5 or 6 days

Difficulty

Moderate

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Marangu Gate to Mandara Hut

  • Elevation: 6,000 ft (1,830 m) to 8,900 ft (2,720 m)
  • Distance: 8.5 km (5.3 miles)
  • Hike Time: 3-4 hours
  • Accommodation: Mandara Hut
  • Description: The trail passes through lush rainforest, with beautiful scenery and the possibility of seeing wildlife such as monkeys.

Day 2: Mandara Hut to Horombo Hut

  • Elevation: 8,900 ft (2,720 m) to 12,200 ft (3,720 m)
  • Distance: 11.8 km (7.3 miles)
  • Hike Time: 5-7 hours
  • Accommodation: Horombo Hut
  • Description: The trail transitions from rainforest to moorland, with stunning views of Mawenzi Peak and the surrounding landscape.

Day 3: Horombo Hut to Kibo Hut

  • Elevation: 12,200 ft (3,720 m) to 15,500 ft (4,700 m)
  • Distance: 10.5 km (6.5 miles)
  • Hike Time: 4-5 hours
  • Accommodation: Kibo Hut
  • Description: The trail continues through the alpine desert, with increasingly challenging terrain. This day is crucial for acclimatization.

Day 4: Kibo Hut to Summit (Uhuru Peak) and back to Horombo Hut

  • Elevation: 15,500 ft (4,700 m) to 19,341 ft (5,895 m) and back to 12,200 ft (3,720 m)
  • Distance: 12 km (7.5 miles) to the summit and 12 km (7.5 miles) back
  • Hike Time: 6-8 hours to the summit and 4-5 hours back
  • Accommodation: Horombo Hut
  • Description: This is the most challenging day. The ascent to the summit starts around midnight to reach Uhuru Peak by sunrise. The descent back to Horombo Hut is steep and tiring.

Day 5: Horombo Hut to Marangu Gate

  • Elevation: 12,200 ft (3,720 m) to 6,000 ft (1,830 m)
  • Distance: 18 km (11.2 miles)
  • Hike Time: 5-6 hours
  • Description: The final descent through the rainforest back to Marangu Gate. This is a long day, but the excitement of completing the climb makes it enjoyable.

Advantages

  • Accommodation: The Marangu Route is the only route with hut accommodation, providing shelter and basic facilities.
  • Popularity: It is well-trodden, with good infrastructure and frequent rescue operations if needed.
  • Scenic: Offers diverse landscapes from rainforest to alpine desert.

Disadvantages

  • Crowded: Due to its popularity, the route can be crowded, especially during peak seasons.
  • Less Time for Acclimatization: The 5-day itinerary can be challenging for acclimatization compared to longer routes.

Tips for Success

  • Acclimatization: Consider taking an extra day at Horombo Hut to acclimatize.
  • Pace: Take your time and hike at a comfortable pace.
  • Hydration: Stay hydrated, especially at higher altitudes.
  • Weather: Be prepared for changing weather conditions.

If you're planning to climb Kilimanjaro, the Marangu Route is a great option, especially if you prefer a shorter and more structured climb.
