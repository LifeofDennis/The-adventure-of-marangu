Safari Package Overview

Our safari packages offer a variety of experiences, ranging from wildlife viewing in national parks to cultural tours and adventure activities. Here are our most popular safari options:

Classic Wildlife Safari

3-7 days

Locations: Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Tarangire, and Lake Manyara

  • 4x4 safari vehicles with pop-up roofs
  • Tented camps or lodges
  • Full board meals
  • Daily game drives

Highlights: See the Big Five, spectacular landscapes, and cultural interactions

Luxury Safari Experience

5-10 days

Locations: Private conservancies and premium lodges

  • Private transfers and charter flights
  • Luxury lodges with private pools
  • Gourmet dining
  • Hot air balloon safaris

Highlights: Exclusive experiences, personalized service, and ultimate comfort

Family Safari Adventure

5-7 days

Locations: Family-friendly parks and lodges

  • Child-friendly activities
  • Lodges with pools
  • Educational programs
  • Guided bush walks

Highlights: Safe wildlife viewing, educational experiences, and family bonding

Safari & Beach Combo

10-14 days

Locations: Northern Circuit + Zanzibar

  • Wildlife game drives
  • Beachfront resorts
  • Snorkeling and diving
  • Spa treatments

Highlights: Best of both worlds - adventure and relaxation

Safari Planning Tips

Best Time to Visit

June-October for wildlife viewing, January-February for calving season

What to Pack

Neutral-colored clothing, binoculars, camera, sunscreen, and comfortable shoes

Health & Safety

Malaria prophylaxis recommended, comprehensive travel insurance advised

Photography

Bring zoom lenses (200-400mm ideal), extra batteries, and memory cards
